## 中文版

### 一、我们收集的信息

**1. 训练数据（仅存储在您的设备本地）**

本应用在您的设备本地存储训练记录，包括：

- 游戏方格尺寸与完成时长
- 点击序列与区域分布数据
- 专注力分析结果（注意力类型、得分）

所有训练数据均经过 AES-256 加密后存储在您的设备上。

**2. iCloud 同步（可选）**

如您的设备已登录 iCloud，训练记录将通过 Apple CloudKit 同步至您的个人 iCloud 账户。此数据仅属于您，我们无权访问。

**3. 广告相关数据**

本应用使用 Google AdMob 展示广告。AdMob 可能收集：

- 广告标识符（IDFA），仅在您通过 App Tracking Transparency 授权后
- 设备类型、系统版本等非个人标识信息

您可随时在"设置 → 隐私与安全性 → 跟踪"中修改授权。

**4. 应用偏好设置**

语言选择、振动开关、广告开关等偏好仅保存在本地，不上传至任何服务器。

### 二、我们不收集的信息

- 不收集姓名、邮箱、手机号等个人身份信息
- 不设置账户或登录系统
- 不向第三方出售您的任何数据
- 无法访问您 iCloud 中的数据

## English Version

### 1. Information We Collect

**a) Training Data (stored locally on your device only)**

Your training records are stored locally on your device, including:

- Grid size and completion time
- Tap sequences and spatial distribution data
- Attention analysis results (type, score)

All training data is encrypted with AES-256 before being stored.

**b) iCloud Sync (Optional)**

If you are signed in to iCloud, your training records may be synced to your personal iCloud account via Apple CloudKit. This data belongs solely to you — we have no access to it.

**c) Advertising Data**

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

- Advertising Identifier (IDFA), only after you grant permission via App Tracking Transparency
- Non-personal device signals such as device type and OS version

You may change this permission at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

**d) App Preferences**

Your language, vibration, and ad preferences are saved locally only and are never uploaded to any server.

---

### 2. Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or any personal identifiers
- We have no account or login system
- We never sell your data to third parties
- We cannot access your iCloud data

---

### 3. Third-Party Services

This app integrates the following third-party services. Please refer to their respective privacy policies:

- Google AdMob: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Apple CloudKit: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

---

### 4. Data Retention

Training data exists only on your device and in your iCloud account. Uninstalling the app removes all local data. iCloud data can be deleted manually via iCloud settings.

---

### 5. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from minors.

---

### 6. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of significant changes through an in-app notice.
